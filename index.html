About me

I'm a theoretical physics PhD candidate at the IFW in Dresden, where I do research mainly focused on topological insulators and superconductors. I am also particoularly interested in statistical mechanics, in the foundations of quantum mechanics and in the history and philosophy of science.

My other big passions, outside of physics, are drawing, photography and anything sea related (swimming, freediving, scuba diving, surfing...).
